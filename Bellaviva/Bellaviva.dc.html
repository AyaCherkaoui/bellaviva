Our Story

Do beautiful things with your beautiful life.

Bellaviva Nail Bar is a small, welcoming studio in Aurora, Illinois, built on a simple belief: a great manicure is a moment of calm in a busy life.

We pair meticulous technique with genuine warmth, taking the time to understand what you want, using clean, quality products, and finishing every set with care. Whether it's a quick refresh or a celebration with friends, you'll leave feeling looked after.
